Blackjack

  • Each person has 2 cards when new game starts and can have maximum 5 cards
  • Player has $100 to get start and can change the bet during the game
  • 1 corresponds to A, an ace which has a value of either 1 or 11, 2 through 10 correspond to those cards, 11 through 13 correspond to Jack, Queen, and King, which have a value of 10
  • Player loses the bet when he lost, gains the bet when he win, and receives no additional payout when the game ends in a draw
  • Press "cash out" to quit and reload the game at any time
